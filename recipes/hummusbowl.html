Hummus Bowl

Description

This hummus bowl is the perfect lunch to have on a busy work day!

It's very rich in nutrients and it takes less than 10 minutes to make!

Ingredients

  • Hummus
  • Cucumbers
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Olives
  • Spinach
  • Pickled Onions
  • Fresh Pita Bread

Steps

  1. Slice your Cucumbers, Cherry Tomatoes and Olives
  2. Put your freshly made hummus into the bowl
  3. Top it with the cucumber, pickled onion, olives, cherry tomatoes
  4. Put the spinach and the pita bread on the side
  5. Enjoy!
