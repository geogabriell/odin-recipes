Tuna Pasta

Description

This Tuna Pasta recipe is one to die for!

It takes 20 minutes to make and it is the best recipe for an amateur cook!

Ingredients

  • 250g Pasta
  • 1 can Tuna
  • 250ml Tomato Sauce
  • 50g Olives
  • Half an Onion
  • Salt, Pepper, Chilli powder, Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta according to the instructions on the package
  2. Saute the onion and the olives
  3. After 5 minutes, add the Tuna and the Tomato Sauce
  4. After the pasta is done, pour them into the sauce and mix them up!
  5. Enjoy!
